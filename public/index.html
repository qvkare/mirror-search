About Mirror Search

Mirror Search is a privacy-first web search engine built on the Bless Network. We don't track users, store search history, or collect personal data.

Features:

  • Multi-engine search with intelligent fallback
  • Advanced bot protection and human-like behavior
  • Real-time status indicators
  • Fast, secure, and private

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority:

  • No Tracking: We don't use cookies or tracking pixels
  • No Logs: Search queries are not stored or logged
  • No Personal Data: We don't collect IP addresses or user information
  • Secure: All searches are encrypted and anonymous

Mirror Search is designed to be completely anonymous and private.
